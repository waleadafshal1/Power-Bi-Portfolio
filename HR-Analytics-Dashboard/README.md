# HR Workforce & Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of workforce demographics and employee performance. Designed for HR leadership, it enables data-driven decisions regarding recruitment, retention, and employee engagement by visualizing key metrics across various dimensions like department, job level, and geography.

## Tools and Technologies
* **Power BI Desktop**: For data modeling and visualization.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**: For calculating complex KPIs like Turnover Rate and Engagement Index.
* **Microsoft Excel / CSV**: Used as the primary data source for employee records.
* **Figma**: Used for creating the custom dark-themed UI background.

## Dataset
The dataset includes anonymized employee records covering:
* **Demographics**: Age groups, gender, education level, and geographic location (US-based).
* **Employment Details**: Employment type (Full-time/Contract), Salary bands, and Job levels.
* **Performance Metrics**: Productivity scores, Satisfaction ratings, and Manager evaluations.

## Dashboard Structure

### Page 1: Workforce Demographics
**Purpose:** To provide a high-level snapshot of the current workforce composition and identify trends in non-active employees.
* **Key KPIs**: Total Employees (7500), Active Employees (6009), Turnover Rate (19.88%), and Annual Workforce Cost ($204M).
* **Workforce Breakdown**: Distribution by Employment Type (Full-time vs. Part-time) and Salary Bands (Low, Medium, High).
* **Geographic Insights**: A map visual showing the distribution of non-active employees across the United States.
* **Trend Analysis**: A bar chart tracking the growth of non-active employees from 2012 to 2022.

### Page 2: Employee Performance & Engagement
**Purpose:** To evaluate employee health through productivity, satisfaction, and career growth potential.
* **Performance KPIs**: Avg Productivity Score (6.19/10), Avg Satisfaction Score (7.45/10), and Avg Engagement Index (7.43/10).
* **Rating Categories**: Donut charts comparing actual scores against targets for Rating, Satisfaction, and Evaluation.
* **Career Growth Projections**: Analysis of employees based on their potential for growth (Poor to Excellent).
* **Financial Impact**: Monthly bonus distribution across different job levels (Entry through Executive).

## How to Use
1. **Navigation**: Use the sidebar buttons (**Demographics** and **Performance**) to switch between views.
2. **Slicers**: Use the filters on the left (Status, Year, State, Gender, Rating) to drill down into specific employee segments.
3. **Interactive Visuals**: Click on specific segments in the "Age Bucket" or "Job Level" charts to cross-filter the entire page.
